About me

I currently work as a database reporter at the Chronicle of Higher Education. Previously, I've interned at Homicide Watch D.C., the St. Louis Post-Dispatch and the Jerusalem Post. My writing has also appeared in Thirty Two Magazine. I'm a graduate of the Medill School of Journalism at Northwestern University.

I believe investigative journalism is a cornerstone of a strong democracy and empathic reporting can be a powerful tool for bringing people together. I hold dearly to the ideal that learning about the world beyond our borders is crucial to understanding our neighbors next door.
