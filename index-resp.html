About Me

I currently work as a database reporter at the Chronicle of Higher Education. Previously, I've interned at Homicide Watch D.C., the St. Louis Post-Dispatch and the Jerusalem Post. My writing has also appeared in Thirty Two Magazine.

My current focus is on data-driven stories that investigate, explore and, ultimately, complicate our understanding of the world of higher education. As part of The Chronicle's Data and Interactives team, I'm always looking for new ways to draw people into a story and convey information simply, powerfully and beautifully.

I'm a graduate of the Medill School of Journalism at Northwestern University, a proud Minnesotan and an aspiring world traveler.
